THE 2 MOST LEGENDARY PRODUCERS, PIONEERS OF THE MUSIC EDITING ARTFORM AND FREESTYLE ARTISTS DUO OF ALL TIME

The Latin Rascals, consisting of Tony Moran and Johnny "Wild" Perez, are legendary producers and artists who played a pivotal role in the Latin freestyle and dance music scenes of the 1980s and 1990s. Known for their groundbreaking remixes and productions, they blended Latin rhythms with electronic dance beats, shaping the sound of the era. Their remixes of hits by artists like Lisa Lisa & Cult Jam and Stevie B helped bring Latin freestyle to the mainstream. The Latin Rascals’ innovative work left a lasting impact on both Latin pop and dance music, making them one of the most influential and iconic duos in music history.

Tony Moran is a renowned music producer, remixer, and artist known for his groundbreaking work in the Latin freestyle and dance music scenes. He is also the creative force behind the project Concept of One, where he collaborates with some of the most famous artists in the music industry. As a producer and remixer, Tony Moran has worked with a wide range of iconic artists, blending Latin, pop, and electronic dance music to create unforgettable hits. His collaborations have included working with major stars like Mariah Carey, P!nk, Shakira, and Jennifer Lopez. His ability to infuse mainstream pop with Latin influences has cemented his status as one of the most influential figures in contemporary music. Through Concept of One, Moran continues to push boundaries, producing innovative music that resonates across genres.
